# [0.31] - 2026-06-30
## Added
#### Spells
- **Bulwark** — Increases your armor based on your current armor and spell power.
- **Taunt** — Taunts all enemies within the spell's radius, forcing them to target you.
- **Sworn Protector** — Redirects a percentage of damage from nearby players to yourself.

#### WIP Spells
- **Bedrock Skin** (WIP) — Immobilizes you while granting tank buffs (Armor, Max HP, Resistance).
- **Ram** (WIP) — Dashes through enemies at short range, with effectiveness scaling with your armor.

- Added mod logo
